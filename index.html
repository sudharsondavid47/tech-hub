    What is VRV?

    What is VRV? Variable Refrigerant Volume.

    VRV intelligently uses one outdoor unit to deliver a precise amount of refrigerant to each indoor unit at a time.

    • Each indoor unit can control comfort independently.
    • Variable refrigerant flow supports efficient part-load operation.
    • Outdoor capacity is shared across connected indoor zones.
    DAIKIN

    Facilitator Notes

    Based on field conditions, VRV varies compressor and refrigerant output instead of sending a fixed amount to every zone.
